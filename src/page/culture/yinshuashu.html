// Priniting Technique

Bi Sheng invented clay type printing in the Northern Song Dynasty

Block printing originated in ancient China. As early as the Sui and Tang Dynasties, block printing in the city. In the Song Dynasty, Bi Sheng invented movable-type printing. Various kinds of movable type appeared in the past dynasties, including clay wood movable type, pottery movable type, porcelain movable type, copper movable type and so on, but movable type printing was not popular in ancient China. There are a large number of Chinese characters, movable type printing is not as convenient as western characters, and the technical requirements are also higher; Woodblock printing is easy to reflect the beauty of Chinese calligraphy, and easy to preserve.
